The aim of the game is simple: you must match pairs of identical cards. You have 5 seconds to memorize a grid of 5x5 cards, and then you both take turns selecting pairs of cards from the now face-down grid. Matching a correct pair will grant you an extra turn.

Be careful though! One of the cards is an 8-ball card! Unless this card is the last one picked, the player who accidentally selects it will immediately lose. The 8-ball card will be highlighted in red up until the cards get turned around, so make sure you remember its position well.
